Newborn Records Management System

About Our System

First Contact CHCC is a comprehensive newborn records management system designed for healthcare professionals.

Our system helps doctors and hospital staff efficiently manage and track newborn patient records including:

  • Baby information (name, date of birth, gender)
  • Parent details (mother and father names)
  • Contact information (phone, email, address)
  • Complete medical history tracking

Access Records

Healthcare professionals can login to access and manage newborn records.
